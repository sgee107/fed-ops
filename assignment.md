# FDE Take-home Assignment

## Overview

How do you think about selling Exa outside of our obvious core markets (tech + finance)? The goal here isn't to be right but instead to see how you think: how you simplify a complex product and how you identify creative ways Exa could grow into places most people wouldn't even look.

Pick a market that's not tech or finance — whatever feels interesting to you. Walk us through:

1. What challenges their IT, sales, sourcing teams or any team in that space face today?
2. What needs their product(s) might have, and any additions they might want?
3. What kind of pilot or proof of concept you'd run to get them bought in?
4. Why do you think most vendors ignore it?
5. What signals or data do you think Exa could uniquely capture that others can't? Because of this, what is the market we can capture?
6. What barriers would you expect in the sales process, and how would you overcome them?

Generally, why is this a compelling market for Exa to go after?

## What to Build

Build a small applet that demonstrates a concrete use case in your chosen market, powered by Exa's API.

Your applet should:

- Use Exa's API to retrieve, search, or analyze real external content relevant to your chosen market
- Make it clear who the end user is and what problem they are solving
- Show how Exa's capabilities would fit into that user's actual workflow

## What to Write

Along with the applet, include a one-page write-up that explains your market choice and value proposition using the questions above as a guide.

## Deliverables

- A working URL to your deployed applet or short demo video walking through your applet
- A GitHub repository link or a zip of your source code
- Your market choice and value proposition address the questions

Let us know if you have any questions!
